Tiffin's Indian Restaurant

At Tiffin's, we serve fresh, fast and flavorful Indian food, freshly whipped lassis, and select wine and beer. We currently have locations in Boulder, Denver and Louisville.

Tiffin's is committed to making affordable and healthy Indian cuisine and offer a variety of Vegan & Gluten Free Menu Items. Our entire menu is priced under $10. At Tiffin's, you can dine in, take out or have our food delivered through Dine on Demand, a Louisville based restaurant delivery service.
